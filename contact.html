Urgent Material Needs

Beyond fiscal support, our children are constantly in need of physical supplies to prevent school dropouts and ensure stable health. We gladly accept:

  1. Scholastic materials (books, pens, mathematical sets).
  2. School uniforms and standard footwear.
  3. Bedding materials, mosquito nets, and clothing.
  4. Agricultural inputs and seeds for our sustainable farms.
  5. Clean home hygiene kits and medical first-aid supplies.

Accountability Guarantee

At El-Shaddai Orphanage Foundation, resource tracking and transparency serve as our operating foundation. Every single contribution received goes directly to field initiatives in Usuk sub-county, managed through explicit performance tracking benchmarks to guarantee maximum real-world impact.

Partner With Us & Save Futures

Step forward to support orphans, protect vulnerable girl-children, and uplift isolated communities in Eastern Uganda.

In Katakwi District, vulnerable children confront severe, life-altering challenges. Following parental loss to complications from HIV/AIDS, many young souls are left totally isolated—facing deep social stigma, severe starvation, and economic exploitation. Without systemic help, children are forced to leave school to perform exhausting manual labor just to feed their siblings.

Your support breaks this tragic cycle. By partnering with El-Shaddai Orphanage Foundation, you directly provide safe housing, mental health rehabilitation, clinical medical therapy, and quality academic placement. Choose your preferred channel below to instantly connect with our outreach team.
